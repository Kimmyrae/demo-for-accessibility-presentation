All about Cats

A website devoted to internet cats

Maru

Maru is an adorable little Scottish Fold from Japan. She started becoming popular on YouTube when her owners started uploading videos of her.

Honey Bee, The Blind Hiking Cat

Honey Bee is an amazing blind cat that loves to go hiking!

Grumpy Cat

Grumpy Cat is arguably one of the most successful cats (if not already the number one cat) on the web today.

Pusheen

Pusheen is a cartoon cat who is the subject of comic strips and sticker sets on Facebook. Pusheen Cat was created in 2010 by Claire Belton and Andrew Duff for a comic strip on their website, Everyday Cute.

Lil Bub

Runt-of-the-litter Lil Bub started making waves on the web in November of 2011 after photos of her were uploaded to social sites like Tumblr and Reddit.
